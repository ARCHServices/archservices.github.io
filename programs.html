Trust House Shelter Programs

Heroes Refuge: 15-bed shelter dedicated to homeless male veterans

Safe Refuge: 12-bed shelter dedicated to homeless individuals, women and families.

Our 27-bed facility is located in Old Southwest Roanoke, VA and provides the initial stabilization needed for clients to begin the process of exiting the cycle of homelessness. We provide therapeutic case management and life skills classes that include, but are not limited to: financial health management (budgeting, fiscal responsibility, etc.), physical and mental health management, and more. We also provide referrals to other community agencies to ensure the client is connected to the appropriate resources needed.

Housing Stability Program

New Beginnings: This program provides the supportive services that are set in place for clients once they enter into housing to ensure that they remain housed. This includes therapeutic case management in the clients’ home, and support to include: advocacy, community resource referrals, transportation to appointments if needed and more.

Peer Supported Recovery Houses

Stepping Stones: Our PSRH program is the product of a partnership between ARCH and Renovation Alliance, another local nonprofit. These are houses that have been re-furbished, made safe for living, and are sub- leased (under the fair-market rate) for clients to live together (affordably) after transitioning out of shelter offering the support and accountability needed to live successfully in the community.

Permanent Supportive Housing

Heroes Haven: The PSH program of Trust House is designed specifically for chronically homeless veterans and is scattered throughout the community. Using funding from the Department of Housing and Urban Development, this program provides housing subsidies while surrounding the clients with the supportive services of the Housing Stability Coordinator. This aids in the success of the clients remaining housed and therefore being a productive member of the community.



Residential Substance Abuse Treatment

Bethany Hall: This program is also located in Old Southwest Roanoke and provides residential substance abuse treatment and a second chance for women, pregnant women, and recently postpartum women in recovery from drugs and alcohol. We offer a holistic approach to recovery and provide a supportive and healing treatment environment. In operation for the last 45 years, this program is pivotal in the success of women’s recovery as well as the health of their unborn babies.


ABOUT US

With a combined history of approximately 90 years, Trust House and Bethany Hall have become programs of ARCH to increase capacity and provide the best possible care with the least amount of barriers to the most vulnerable populations in our community.
