Tea for Trust

Join us Sunday April 24 for our third annual Tea for Trust.

We will be serving delicious teas and finger foods with entertainment. There will also be a featured guest speaker. As always we will be hosting a silent auction with a variety of goods and services to bid on.

Ticket information: Tickets are $35 and can be bought online through by clicking “Donate”, entering your information, and notating “Tea for Trust tickets” in the notes section. Your name will be on a list at the event entrance when you purchase tickets online. If you would like a hard copy ticket, please contact Rachel Moore, Executive Assistant at 540.344.8060 x 108 or at rachel.moore@archservices.org. Please note that hard copy tickets are limited.
